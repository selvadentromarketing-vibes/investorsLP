# **SLVD Investor Page - PRD**

## **Overview**
A high-performance, bilingual real estate investment landing page showcasing Selvadentro Tulum properties. Designed to educate investors about premium Tulum properties and drive lead generation through multiple conversion funnels.

## **Target Users**
- International real estate investors (primary: North America, Europe)
- High-net-worth individuals seeking Tulum properties
- Users interested in luxury beachfront or resort developments
- Spanish and English-speaking prospects
- Mobile and desktop visitors

## **Core Features**
- Interactive property galleries with lightbox viewing
- Bilingual content (English/Spanish language toggle)
- Multiple lead-capture funnels (book call, guide download, webinar signup)
- Investment comparison tables and project timelines
- Amenities showcase with hover effects
- Investor testimonials and track record section
- Sticky mobile CTA bar for lead capture on mobile devices
- Scroll-triggered animations and reveal effects
- Performance-optimized images and lazy loading
- Meta Pixel integration for conversion tracking

## **Tech Stack**
- **Frontend:** HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4.2.2
- **Analytics:** Meta Pixel
- **Hosting:** Netlify CDN
- **Fonts:** Google Fonts (Cardo, Work Sans, Lexend)

## **Future Enhancements**
- CRM integration for lead management automation
- Live chat support for real-time investor questions
- Video tours of properties and amenities
- Investment calculator tool for projected returns
- Multi-language expansion (Portuguese, French)
- Client dashboard for investor portfolio tracking
